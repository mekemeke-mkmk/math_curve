This add-in allows you to generate advanced mathematical curves directly in Autodesk Fusion sketches.

- Parametric curves
- High precision curve generation

## Privacy Policy

Math Curve
Create Mathematical Curves Directly in Fusion 360
Math Curve is a Fusion 360 add-in that allows you to generate sketch geometry from mathematical equations. Create complex curves, spirals, waveforms, and artistic patterns that would be difficult or time-consuming to draw manually.

Whether you're an engineer, designer, educator, student, or maker, Math Curve makes it easy to bring mathematical shapes into your CAD workflow.

Features
Generate sketch curves from mathematical equations

Support for parametric equations

Real-time curve preview

Adjustable resolution for smooth, accurate results

Creates native Fusion 360 sketch geometry

Fast and intuitive workflow

Ideal For
Engineering and product design

Mathematical visualization

STEM education

Generative and computational design

Artistic and decorative patterns

3D printing projects

Examples
Create a wide variety of mathematical curves, including:

Sine and cosine waves

Spirals

Lissajous curves

Rose curves

Heart-shaped curves

Custom parametric designs

Easy to Use
Simply enter your equation, adjust the parameters, and generate the curve. The resulting geometry becomes a standard Fusion 360 sketch that can be edited, extruded, patterned, and integrated into your existing designs.

Transform mathematical ideas into manufacturable geometry with just a few clicks.
